\section{Introduction}
Modern AI research has shifted towards specialized architectures. 
For instance, the foundation of modern sequence modeling was established by the Transformer architecture [1]. 
Since then, developments in computer vision have led to highly efficient segmentation models like SAM 2 [2].

Furthermore, the scale of models is being re-evaluated, with some arguing that small language models are actually the future of agentic workflows [3]. 
This efficiency is particularly critical in specialized domains such as multi-agent financial trading [4] 
or healthcare applications such as Alzheimer's disease classification using sparse transformers [5].
